A visual journey exploring memories, places, and the traces left behind by our travels.

The Photographers

Katerina Apostolidi

Katerina Apostolidi is a photographer based in Athens, Greece. Her work explores themes of memory, place, and the passage of time through intimate observations of everyday life.

With a background in visual arts, Katerina's photographic practice focuses on capturing the subtle poetry found in ordinary moments. Her images invite viewers to slow down and contemplate the relationships between people, spaces, and memories.

Her work has been exhibited in galleries across Europe and published in various photography journals and magazines.

Kostas Tsanakas

Kostas Tsanakas is a documentary photographer whose work examines the intersection of landscape, culture, and personal narrative. Based in Greece, his photographs reflect a deep engagement with the environments he encounters.

Through his lens, Kostas captures the essence of places and the stories they hold, creating visual narratives that speak to both the universal and the particular aspects of human experience.

His collaborative projects have been featured in international exhibitions, and his work continues to explore the ways photography can preserve and reimagine collective memory.

The Curator

Danae Nagel

Danae Nagel is an independent curator and art critic specializing in contemporary photography and visual culture. With extensive experience in curating exhibitions that explore the relationship between image-making and collective memory, she brings a thoughtful perspective to photographic narratives.

Her curatorial practice is characterized by a deep interest in how photography mediates our understanding of place, identity, and time. She has worked with numerous galleries and cultural institutions, developing exhibitions that create meaningful dialogues between artists and audiences.

Through her work, Danae seeks to create spaces where photography can be experienced not just as documentation, but as a medium for reflection, conversation, and connection.
